# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) - Design Notes

## Logo Design Rationale

### Color Palette
The logo employs the requested orange, white, and black palette:
- **Primary Orange (#ff6b35)**: Represents energy, innovation, and technological advancement. It conveys action and forward-thinking in the context of digital risk.
- **Black**: Symbolizes structure, security, and authority. It grounds the design and conveys the serious, professional nature of digital risk management.
- **White**: Provides clarity and accessibility, ensuring legibility at all sizes from favicon to large displays.

### Geometric Shape - Isometric Cube
The cube-inspired geometric form serves three symbolic functions:
1. **Structure**: Cubes represent stability, systematic thinking, and foundational architecture—essential to risk management frameworks.
2. **Resilience**: The three-dimensional form suggests multi-faceted strength and the ability to withstand pressures from multiple angles.
3. **Risk & Complexity**: The interconnected planes represent the layered, interconnected nature of digital and cyber risk in modern organizations.

### Design Characteristics
- **Minimalist & Tech-Forward**: Clean lines and geometric abstraction align with contemporary tech and education sectors.
- **Legibility at Small Sizes**: The cube design maintains clarity even when scaled down to favicon (16px) or mobile header sizes (32px).
- **Two Variants**:
  - **Icon-Only**: Standalone cube for app icons, favicons, and social media profiles.
  - **Icon + Text**: Full logo with "Institute of Digital Risk" for primary branding and formal communications.

## Homepage Design Philosophy

### Visual Hierarchy & Layout
- **Hero Section**: Bold, dark background with geometric accent to capture attention and communicate the institute's modern, tech-forward positioning.
- **Sticky Navigation**: Ensures accessibility to key sections throughout the user journey.
- **Service Cards**: Clean grid layout with hover interactions to invite exploration of IDR's core offerings.
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Enhances user experience and creates a cohesive, modern feel.

### Color Usage
- Primary Orange accents used strategically for CTAs, hover states, and key highlights to maintain visual consistency and guide user action.
- Black and white provide professional contrast, ensuring excellent readability and accessibility.

### Responsiveness
- Mobile-first design adapts seamlessly from 480px to desktop, with collapsible navigation and reordered layouts to maintain usability.
- Forms, service cards, and community lists adapt intelligently to screen size.

### Accessibility Features
- Semantic HTML5 elements (header, nav, main, section, footer)
- Clear color contrast ratios exceeding WCAG AA standards
- Smooth focus states on all interactive elements
- Form labels properly associated with inputs
- Skip-to-section functionality via navigation buttons
